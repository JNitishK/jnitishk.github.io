For me, doodling is more than just drawing. It is a rhythm of focus and calm. Each line I put on paper helps me slow down, clear my head, and rediscover balance in the middle of a busy day. What starts as a casual sketch often grows into something unexpected, carrying pieces of imagination, curiosity, and emotion.

This gallery is a collection of those moments, where creativity meets mindfulness and where art becomes both an escape and a source of inspiration.
